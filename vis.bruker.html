Bruker Corporation

Innovation with Integrity

The Bruker Corporation focuses on high–performance scientific instruments and analytical solutions that support medical and life science research, monitor production, improve safety, and protect the environment. Our contributions to sustainability and conservation, as well as to the health and safety of our employees, are key interests and commitments of Bruker.

From its beginning in 1960, Bruker has been driven to provide the best technological solution for each analytical task. Today, thousands of employees in 90 countries continue to work toward this goal.

Bruker systems cover a broad spectrum of applications in all fields of research and development, and are also used in a wide variety of production processes to help ensure process reliability and product quality.

Working globally but thinking locally, Bruker also takes an interest in improving the quality of life in its local communities, and is proud to support the Fitchburg Public Library.
